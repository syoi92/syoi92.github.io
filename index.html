Biography

I am currently a research fellow at Institute of Construction and Environmental Engineering (ICEE). I finished my MEng in SPatial INformatics Group at Seoul National University, advised by Yongil Kim and Kiyun Yu. I got my BE in architectural engineering at Seoul National Univeristy in South Korea.

As an early startup team member, I am also participating in UITI [ ✉️] based on LBS business in Korea. I engaged in launching 집현전 [Wise Strategy to Buy a House], the web service that recommends real estate and links customers to the agents. Besides, by proposing a business item recommending commercial district to new business owners, I won a government fund worth $50000 in my sole name. For do that, I’ve been working on a minimum value product with the team I’ve hired.

I plan to be an incoming student for the doctoral program in CEE at Georgia Tech from 2021 Spring.
