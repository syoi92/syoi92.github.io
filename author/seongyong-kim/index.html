Graduate Research Assistant

Georgia Technology of Institute

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering at Georgia Tech. Being advised by Dr. Yong K Cho, I’m working in Robotic & Intelligence Construction Automation Lab (RICAL). My research interest is centered on Construction Automation based on UAV & UGV, 3D scene understanding, and GIS.

I received a BE in Architectural Engineering and a MS in Civil Engineering both at Seoul National University. I worked in SPatial INformatics group under the guidance of Yongil Kim and Kiyun Yu. After then, as an early startup team member of UITI[ ✉️], I engaged in launching a Korean web service of 집현전 [Wise Strategy to Buy a House].
